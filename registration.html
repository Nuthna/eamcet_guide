Step-by-Step Video Guide

Registration Process Overview

1. Visit Official Website

Go to the official EAMCET counseling website and click on the registration link.

2. Fill Personal Details

Enter your personal information, EAMCET hall ticket number, and other required details.

3. Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of required documents in the specified format.

4. Fee Payment

Pay the counseling processing fee through online payment methods.

5. Print Registration Form

Download and print the registration form for future reference.

Important Notes

  • Keep all original documents ready for verification
  • Save your registration number for future reference
  • Complete the process before the deadline
  • Keep proof of payment safe
