1. Web Options Entry

What is it?

Choosing the colleges and courses in the order of preference.

How to do it?

  • Log in to the official counseling website using the credentials received during registration or verification.
  • Select the district â†’ College â†’ Branch (e.g., CSE, ECE).
  • Arrange the choices in your preferred order.
  • You can choose as many options as you want.
  • Save and log out.

Important Tips:

  • Choose more options to increase your chances.
  • Arrange choices carefully; higher preferences should be on top.
  • Do it within the given dates.

2. Freezing/Locking the Web Options

  • Once satisfied with your choice list, lock the options before the deadline.
  • Take a printout of the option entry form after locking.
  • If not locked manually, the system will automatically freeze the last saved options after the deadline.

3. Seat Allotment Result

  • On the allotment date, log in to the portal to download the allotment order.
  • It will mention the college and course allotted.
  • You'll also get instructions for reporting to the college.

4. Tuition Fee Payment

  • If allotted a seat, pay the tuition fee (if not covered under fee reimbursement).
  • Fee details will be available in the allotment order.

5. Self-Reporting through Website

  • After fee payment, complete self-reporting online by clicking the "Accept My Joining" option.
  • Download and print the joining report.

6. Report to the Allotted College

Go to the college physically before the deadline mentioned.

Carry:

  • Allotment Order
  • Joining Report
  • Original Documents (as already verified)
  • Tuition Fee Payment Receipt (if paid)
