Certificate Verification Process

Complete guide for document verification at Help Line Center (HLC)

Step-by-Step Video Guide

What Happens in Certificate Verification?

1. Token Issuance

On arriving at the center, students get a token number for their turn.

2. Biometric Verification

Fingerprint or Aadhaar verification may be done to ensure identity.

3. Document Verification

Officials verify original documents one by one. If anything is missing or incorrect, they will inform the student.

4. Caste/Income Verification

If the student claims a reservation (SC/ST/BC/EWS), the relevant certificates are verified.

5. Verification Completion

Once done, the student receives a Verified Certificate List / Receipt of Certificates with a login password.
