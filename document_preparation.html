Required Documents

Essential documents needed for EAMCET counseling process

Mandatory Documents

EAMCET Hall Ticket Original hall ticket used for the examination
EAMCET Rank Card Original rank card with your score and rank
Intermediate/10+2 Mark Sheet Original marks memo with pass certificate
SSC/10th Marks memo Original marks memo with pass certificate
Transfer Certificate Original TC from your last institution
Study Certificate Certificate showing your study details
Aadhar Card Original Aadhar card for identity proof
Passport Size Photos 2 recent color photographs (35mm x 45mm)

Additional Documents (If Applicable)

Caste Certificate For SC/ST/OBC candidates Optional
Income Certificate For fee reimbursement Optional
PH/NCC/Sports Certificate For special category candidates Optional

Important Notes

All documents must be original and in good condition

Keep 2-3 photocopies of each document

Photocopies should be self-attested

Documents should be arranged in the order listed above
