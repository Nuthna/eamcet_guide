  1. What is EAMCET Counseling?
    2. EAMCET counseling is the process through which qualified candidates are allotted seats in engineering, agriculture, and medical colleges based on their ranks, preferences, and seat availability.
  2. What are the steps involved in EAMCET counseling?
    3. Step 1: Payment of Counseling Fee
    Step 2: Certificate Verification
    Step 3: Slot Booking (For certificate verification)
    Step 4: Web Options Entry (Selecting colleges & branches)
    Step 5: Seat Allotment
    Step 6: Reporting to the college
  3. Where can I register for EAMCET counseling?
    4. Visit the official website: AP EAMCET: https://eapcet-sche.aptonline.in
    TS EAMCET: https://tseamcet.nic.in
  4. What documents are required for EAMCET counseling?
    5. EAMCET Rank Card
    EAMCET Hall Ticket
    SSC (10th) & Intermediate (12th) Marks Memo
    Transfer Certificate (TC)
    Aadhar Card
    Caste Certificate (if applicable)
    Income Certificate (if applicable)
    Residence Certificate
    Local/Non-local Status Certificate
  5. How do I book a slot for certificate verification?
    6. Log in with your details on the official website, pay the counseling fee, and choose a convenient date & center for verification.
  6. What is Web Options Entry?
    7. In this step, you select your preferred colleges and branches in order of priority. This will be considered during the seat allotment.
  7. Can I change my Web Options after submitting them?
    8. Yes, you can modify your web options within the given window for changes before the final lock-in.
  8. What if I forget to lock my Web Options?
    9. If you don‚Äôt lock them, the system will auto-lock your latest saved preferences when the deadline ends.
  9. How is seat allotment done?
    10. Seat allotment is based on: Your EAMCET rank
    Category (OC/BC/SC/ST etc.)
    Local area
    Gender
    Reservation rules
    Availability of seats in your preferred college & branch
  10. Where will I see my seat allotment result?
    11. The result will be displayed on the official website under your login. You can also download the Allotment Order from there.
  11. What should I do after seat allotment?
    12. Download the Allotment Letter
    Pay the tuition fee (if applicable)
    Report to the college within the given time
  12. Can I participate in 2nd/3rd phase counseling if I miss the 1st?
    13. Yes, but you will be eligible only for the remaining available seats.
  13. Can I change the allotted college in the next round?
    14. Yes, you can participate in further rounds and slide to a better option if available.
  14. Is the counseling process the same for Agriculture & Medical courses?
    15. It is similar, but conducted separately by the respective departments (sometimes through different websites).
  15. How can I get help during the counseling process?
    16. You can contact: Helpline centers listed on the official site Toll-free numbers or email IDs mentioned for support
